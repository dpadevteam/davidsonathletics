About Us
Davidson Athletics is a clothing manufacturer and retailer, specializing in the sportswear market, offering both ready-to-wear and custom-made sublimation clothing products, including but not limited to, sportswear, uniforms, and themed outfits.
We are located in Sitio Ubos, Poblacion, Barangay Poblacion, Cebu City, Philippines, PH 6000.
Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
Q: Do you create clothing designs?
A: Yes, we can create designs based on your needs, but if you have your own designs, we accept them as well.
Q: What types of fabric do you use?
A: We use Polydex, a synthetic fabric composed of polyester and Dextron nylon. Polydex is smooth, lightweight, absorbent, quick-drying, and resistant to abrasion, making it ideal for creating high-quality sportswear.
Q: How long would it take?
A: Our custom-made bundles would be completed and ready to ship within one to two weeks.
Q: What are the payment methods available?
A: We accept credit cards, bank transfer, and GCash.
